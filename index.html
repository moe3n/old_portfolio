Mahmudul Hasan is a Bangladesh based photographer who specialises in black and white photography. His work is often abstract and incorporates elements of surrealism, geometry, high contrast and the realities and diversities of human life.

Apart from being a photoghrapher, Mahmud is currently taking part in the Bachelor in Computer Science and Engineering program in the renowned United International University. He is expected to be a graduate by somewhat around 2022.
