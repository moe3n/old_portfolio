Mahmudul Hasan is a Bangladesh based photographer who greatly appreciates the black and white medium. Through his photographic eyes, he often tries to explore the different areas of human condition and elements of surrealism in abstract form.

In addition to being a small-time photoghrapher, Mahmud is currently doing his Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from the reputed United International University. He is expected to be an able graduate by somewhere around 2022.
